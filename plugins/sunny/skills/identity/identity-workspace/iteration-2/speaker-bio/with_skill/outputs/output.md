Sunny Kolattukudy is a Staff Engineer at Verifiable, a healthcare credentialing SaaS, where he leads the Developer Experience guild, mentors engineers, and owns source automation at the core of the product.

His path here wasn't straight. He completed most of his clinical rotations at Case Western Reserve University's Cleveland Clinic Lerner College of Medicine before leaving in his fourth year — not because it wasn't going well, but because the system made it harder to help people, not easier. That instinct for the end-user never left.

Between medicine and software he co-founded and recorded an album with Deadiron, a thrash metal band. It turns out building a locked-in band and building a functional engineering team require the same thing: no solos.
