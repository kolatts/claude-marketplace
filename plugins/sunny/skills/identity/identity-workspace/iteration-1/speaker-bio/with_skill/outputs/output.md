Sunny Kolattukudy spent four years in medical school before walking away in his fourth year — not because it wasn't working, but because the system made it hard to actually help people. Between medicine and software, he co-founded a heavy metal band and recorded an album. He's been building software for a decade since, currently as an IC and technical leader at Verifiable, a healthcare credentialing SaaS where the clinical background turns out to be genuinely useful. He also runs Imagile, an AI-forward software consulting practice. He builds the way he makes music: direct, collaborative, with an eye on what the whole thing is doing.
